    def semiring_lang  :
    lang

    A semiring is a {×, +, 0, 1}-structure which satisfies the identities

    1. u + (v + w) = (u + v) + w
    2. u + v = v + u
    3. u + 0 = u
    4. u × (v × w) = (u × v) × w
    5. u × 1 = u, 1 × u = u
    6. u × (v + w) = (u × v) + (u × w)
    7. (v + w) × u = (v × u) + (w × u)
    Equations

    def ring_lang  :
    lang

    A ring is a {×,+,−,0,1}-structure which satisfies the identities

    1. u + (v + w) = (u + v) + w
    2. u + v = v + u
    3. u + 0 = u
    4. u + (−u) = 0
    5. u × (v × w) = (u × v) × w
    6. u × 1 = u, 1 × u = u
    7. u × (v + w) = (u × v) + (u × w)
    8. (v + w) × u = (v × u) + (w × u)
